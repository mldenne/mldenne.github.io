About Me

In the middle of my life, I decided to take the road less travelled. So I did something totally unexpected and enrolled in an immersive coding school, otherwise known as the Iron Yard.
Some would say this is a crazy thing to do. I agree. But I also think roads less traveled offer insights that make life much more interesting.
I've worked in several different industries as an administrative assistant, executive assistant, travel coordinator and customer service representative. Currently, I'm slinging burgers at a popular burger joint, and while I really enjoy what I do, I made the decision to move on to something radically different...and become a developer!

A good way to travel...my final project:
The Indy Public Transit Survey

I love the service public transportation provides, and because of my newly developed skill set, I have the ability to help bring more public transportation options to Indianapolis. How exciting is that?
